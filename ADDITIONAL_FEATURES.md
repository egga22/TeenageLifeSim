# Additional Features — Teenage Life Sim

Beyond the explicitly requested features, the following additional features have been implemented:

## Major Additional Features (15 pts each)

1. **Full NPC Character System** — 10 unique NPCs (Alex, Jordan, Sam, Riley, Morgan, Casey, Ms. Chen, Mr. Garcia, Principal Thompson, Parent) each with distinct personalities, avatars, roles, and dialogue patterns. They appear contextually throughout the story.

2. **Shop / Economy System** — In-game currency ($) with 8 purchasable items (Coffee, Snack, Notebook, Headphones, Gift, Energy Drink, Novel, Video Game) each with unique stat effects and descriptions. Money management adds strategic depth.

3. **Club System** — 6 different clubs (Sports, Drama, Science, Music, Art, Gaming) that players can join at the Club Fair, each providing unique stat bonuses and social opportunities.

4. **Relationship System with NPCs** — Social dynamics with relationship levels (Stranger → Acquaintance → Friend → Good Friend → Best Friend → Inseparable).

5. **What-If Simulator Mode** — A unique mode that lets players replay key decision points to explore alternate outcomes without restarting the full game.

## Minor Additional Features (10 pts each)

1. **Multiple Color Themes** — 6 visual themes (Default, Ocean, Forest, Sunset, Midnight, Pastel) that completely reskin the game's appearance.

2. **Keyboard Navigation** — Full keyboard support including number keys 1-9 for quick choice selection, Escape for menu, and tab navigation.

3. **Save Export/Import** — Players can export their save data as JSON files and import them on different devices, enabling cross-device play.

4. **Character Style & Personality System** — 5 styles (Casual, Preppy, Punk, Athletic, Artistic) and 5 personalities (Outgoing, Shy, Ambitious, Creative, Rebel) with unique starting stat bonuses.

5. **Pronoun Support** — Full pronoun selection (They/Them, He/Him, She/Her) with dynamic text replacement throughout the story.

6. **Toast Notification System** — Animated notifications for stat changes, achievements, events, and game actions.

7. **Dyslexia-Friendly Font Option** — Accessibility toggle for dyslexia-friendly typography with adjusted letter/word spacing.

8. **Moral Dilemma System** — Complex ethical choices (bully intervention, cheating reporting) with meaningful consequences affecting multiple stats.

9. **Day/Night Cycle** — Story progression through Morning → Classes → Lunch → After School → Evening with distinct activities per period.

10. **Dynamic Character Preview** — Real-time avatar and name preview during character creation.

11. **Catch Minigame** — An additional action minigame where players catch falling items while avoiding bombs using mouse/touch controls.

12. **Text Speed Control** — Four text animation speeds (Instant, Fast, Normal, Slow) for the typewriter effect.

13. **Screen Reader Optimization Mode** — Enhanced mode that shows stat labels, adds numbered choice prefixes, and hides decorative elements for better screen reader compatibility.

14. **Reduce Motion Option** — Disables all animations for users with vestibular disorders or motion sensitivity.
